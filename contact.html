Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about municipal services

How can I submit a request online?

You can submit requests through our Citizen Portal. Simply visit the citizen portal, fill out the request form with your details and required information, and submit it online. You'll receive a confirmation email with your request ID for tracking.

How long does it take to process requests?

Processing times vary by request type. Building permits typically take 5-10 business days, business licenses take 3-7 days, and certificates are usually processed within 2-5 days. You can track your request status online using your request ID.

Can I pay municipal fees online?

Yes, we offer secure online payment options for municipal fees, taxes, and fines. You can pay using credit cards, bank transfers, or mobile payment methods. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

How do I report a municipal issue?

You can report issues through our complaint system in the Citizen Portal. Provide detailed information about the issue, upload photos if available, and specify the location. Our team will investigate and resolve the issue within 48 hours.

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. Online services are available 24/7.

How can I participate in community polls?

Active polls are available in the Citizen Portal. Simply visit the polls section, read the question and options, and cast your vote. Your participation helps shape municipal decisions and policies.
